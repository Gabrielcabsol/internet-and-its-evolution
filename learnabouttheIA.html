Theory

Machine learning

The use and development of computer systems that are able to learn and adapt without following explicit instructions, by using algorithms and statistical models to analyze and draw inferences from patterns in data.

Natural language processing

Applications are used to derive insights from unstructured text-based data and give you access to extracted information to generate new understanding of that data. Natural language processing examples can be built using Python, TensorFlow, and PyTorch.

Computer vision

Computer vision is a field of computer science that focuses on enabling computers to identify and understand objects and people in images and videos.

Ethics

There’s a fear about machine learning where people think about the future and are scared that a machine or AI can learn so fast that, in a bad functioning, it can adopt human biases and form its own opinion, which can be dangerous in the future.

Also, AI must handle sensitive data with care, comply with required laws and standards, provide users with clear notice, and give them necessary controls over data use. Best practices like encryption should be followed, adhering to privacy principles.

AI has also impacted jobs and society. Tools like ChatGPT or You.com can be very helpful but also harmful depending on their use. For example, if someone uses AI to cheat or do their work for them, it can harm society by eroding people's capacity to think and reason.
